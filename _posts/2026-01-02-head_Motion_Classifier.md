General use computers and keyboards are commonly made to be used with hands. However, disabled individuals who can ºt extensively use their hands may have trouble using computers. The goal of this project is to use edge AI to detect different head motions of a user to interface with various devices, making for easier use of a computer or application for those who have trouble using their hands
