In the current state, blood pressure monitoring is episodic and intrusive (cuff).
The gap: Hypertension is dynamic -- A snapshot once in a while misses the full picture.
**Our solution:** An embedded deep learning enabled device with the capability of continuous, passive monitoring *using a single optical sensor.*
