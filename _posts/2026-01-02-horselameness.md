Equine lameness is one of the most prevalent health issues for horses, causing early retirement, over $5,000 in average vet costs per case. Currently on the market there are multi-sensor lab rigs that cost over $15K. It is estimated that 2 to 3 million horses a year suffer from lameness. Our device targets amateur and professional riders as well as veterinarians.
