    About Me

    Well, a little bit about me for now, but hopefully more soon...

    Hi, I am an Associate Teaching Professor of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Carnegie Mellon University. I created this website to share my latest contributions. Recently, I am really proud of creating two new ECE courses, Embedded Deep Learning and Autonomous Robotics 1.

    I am also an artist, a maker, and a tinkerer. I hope to find time soon to write on my blog about my journey from a curious child tinkerer growing up in Damascus, Syria, to an engineering faculty member. For now, you can check my faculty page at CMU or get my CV here.
